Tacos

Description

    Ingredients
  • 8 taco shells, hard or soft
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1/2 head lettuce
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 avocado
  • Seasoning
    • 1 tablespoon chili powder
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
    • 1 tablespoon sea salt
    • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    Preparation
  1. Chop lettuce, tomato, onion, and avocado. Set aside
  2. Mix together seasoning ingredients, and set aside. (Note: You need not add all the chili powder and red pepper flakes if you prefer a milder seasoning.)
  3. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F. Add tacos
  4. While tacos are in the oven, cook beef on medium heat for 6 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Add beans to beef, cook another 1-4 minutes
  6. Take beef off heat and drain. Add seasoning
  7. Remove tacos from the oven
  8. Collect and serve the tacos with their fixings!
